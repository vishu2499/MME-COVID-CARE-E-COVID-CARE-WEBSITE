ABOUT US

MM e-Covid care initiative has been taken by two young minds Dr. Shashank Mishra & Dr. Honey Mittal by looking at a burning need of hospital beds and severity of patients due to delayed treatment. Since we the people of India, living in a country with a total population of approximately 135 crores, defeating this pandemic is not an easy task.

Approximately 800 people are dying of this deadly virus called novel corona virus and half of daily confirm cases gets serious or complication arises due to lack of health care facilities, although our government is taking great initiatives to overcome this and we as a medical professionals, using our experience in treating covid-19 patients since the beginning of this pandemic in India have landed up on this idea to start treating Mild and mildly moderate cases at their residence with putting 100% effort so that the severely needy patients gets the benefit of hospital facilities and will lower down the complications due to this virus and daily death figures.

We hope that soon we all going to defeat this Virus and making this Pandemic just a chapter in our History pages.
